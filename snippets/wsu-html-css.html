Washington State University

Washington State University is a public research university dedicated to improving lives by serving the public good. For more than 130 years, we have strived to unlock possibilities by empowering students, faculty, and others to create a world where all people can thrive.

In that spirit, our community welcomes scholars from around the globe and prioritizes equitable opportunities for all.

  • Statewide Reach: 6 campuses, 11 academic colleges, Extension offices in all 39 Washington counties and on the Colville Reservation, 4 research and extension centers
  • A World of Academic Choices: 200+ undergraduate fields of study and nearly 150 graduate and professional degree programs
  • New Solutions to Vexing Challenges: “Very high research spending and doctorate production” designation (Carnegie Classification, 2025)
